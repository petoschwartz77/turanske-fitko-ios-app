# Turanské Fitko App Icon

The production iOS icon is generated during Codemagic builds by `scripts/generate_app_icons.py`.

Design: dark Turanské Fitko background, lime TF monogram, circular energy ring, and Leo paw detail.

Required generated sizes:

- 120 × 120 px
- 180 × 180 px
- 1024 × 1024 px
